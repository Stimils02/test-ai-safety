Graduate Programs

PhD in Computer Science (AI Safety Focus)

Our doctoral program provides rigorous training in AI safety research with world-class faculty mentorship.

  • Full funding guaranteed for 5 years
  • Research rotations in first year
  • Interdisciplinary collaboration opportunities
  • Teaching experience options
Duration: 4-5 years
Application Deadline: December 15
Start Date: Fall Semester
Program Details

Master's in Computer Science (AI Safety Track)

Develop specialized skills in AI safety through our structured Master's program with research and coursework options.

  • Thesis and non-thesis options
  • Industry-relevant curriculum
  • Research assistantships available
  • Professional development workshops
Duration: 1-2 years
Application Deadline: January 15
Start Date: Fall or Spring

Educational Programs

AI Safety Courses

Undergraduate courses focused on AI safety, alignment, and ethics. Available to students from all majors.

  • CS 383: Introduction to AI Safety
  • CS 486: Ethics in Artificial Intelligence
  • CS 490S: Special Topics in AI Alignment
  • PHIL 367: Philosophy of AI
Prerequisites: Vary by course
Credits: 3-4 credits each
Offered: Fall and Spring semesters
Course Catalog

Workshops & Bootcamps

Short-term intensive learning experiences to build skills in AI safety research.

  • Weekend workshops throughout the semester
  • Winter and spring break bootcamps
  • Hands-on technical training
  • No prior experience required for introductory workshops
Duration: 1-5 days
Cost: Free for UMass students
Registration: Required, limited spots
